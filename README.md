## <img src="https://media.giphy.com/media/WFZvB7VIXBgiz3oDXE/giphy.gif" width="28"> About Me

I'm a **4th-year B.Tech Electronics and Communication Engineering** student specializing in **VLSI Design, ASIC Design, and Digital IC Design**. I work at the intersection of RTL, physical design, and silicon — turning specifications into working chips, one flow at a time.

My work spans the **full RTL-to-GDSII journey**: writing and simulating RTL, synthesizing with Cadence Genus, place-and-route with Innovus, and exploring the open-source OpenLane/SKY130 flow as an alternative path to silicon. I care about **reproducibility and documentation** as much as the design itself — I keep detailed, structured records of every flow I run.
